A brand new way to control your health data.

Afia is a blockstack webapp that gives individuals the power to own and control their personal health records (PHR) and securely share them with health care providers upon request.

Send us an email at hello@afiabeta.com


  • Create Afia Account with a Blockstack ID
  • Link HIPPA-compliant data storage of your choice to your Afia account
  • Keep Personal Health Records (PHR) up to date
  • Give read/edit access to selected healthcare providers
  • Export PHR to providers' proprietary systems in the industry standard CCR format

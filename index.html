A brand new way to control your health data.

Say hello to Afia, the Blockstack app that gives individuals the power to own and manage their personal health record. Keep all your important health data encrypted on your cloud storage. Choose the healthcare providers you want to securely share your data with, and revoke access as you see fit.
Your health just got a lot more personal.

Here's how Afia works.

  • Create an Afia account with your Blockstack ID
  • Link a HIPPA-compliant data storage service of your choice
  • Keep your Personal Health Record up to date
  • Give read/edit access to selected healthcare providers
  • Export your PHR in the industry-standard CCR format
